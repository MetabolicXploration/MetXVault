Full Monod for a strain $i$ and resource $j$ is written:

$$\begin{align}
r^{i,j} (c_j) = r_{max}^{i,j} \frac{c_j}{c_j + K_{i,j}}
\end{align}$$

Assuming $K \gg c$

$$\begin{align}
r^{i,j} (c_j) = r_{max}^{i,j} \frac{c_j}{K_{i,j}}
\end{align}$$

defining $\mu_{i,j} \equiv \frac{r_{max}^{i,j}}{K_{i,j}}$

$$\begin{align}
r^{i,j} (c_j) = \mu_{i,j} c_j
\end{align}$$
