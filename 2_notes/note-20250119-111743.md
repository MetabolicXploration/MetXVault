
$$\begin{align}
\omega^2 x^2 + v^2 &= C \\
\frac{k}{m} x^2 + v^2 &= C \\
kx^2 + mv^2 &= mC \\
\frac{1}{2}kx^2 + \frac{1}{2}mv^2 &= \frac{1}{2}mC \\
\end{align}$$

where $\frac{1}{2}kx^2$ is the potential energy of the spring.  See that the rhs is constant.

$$\begin{align}
F = m \ddot{x} &= -kx = - \frac{dV}{dx} \\
V &= \int F dx = k \int x dx \\
V &= \frac{1}{2} k x^2
\end{align}$$
